About YUERSky

Specialists in artificial skylight — nothing else

YUERSky was founded with a single focus: making interior spaces in the Nordic region brighter, healthier, and more human. We believe no one should have to suffer through kaamos in a dark office or a windowless hospital room.

We design, source, and deliver CE and RoHS certified LED skylight panels to interior designers, architects, lighting consultants, wellness operators, and hospitality groups across Finland, Sweden, Norway, and Denmark.

Our panels are not decorative lamps. They are evidence-based human-centric lighting systems — chosen for their clinical-grade colour accuracy (CRI>90), smart circadian control, and proven track record in commercial projects across Europe and Asia.
