Flush-mounted into the ceiling to simulate a real skylight overhead. Creates the immersive sensation of open sky from above — the most powerful light therapy effect.

Window Form

Installed in a wall recess or frame to simulate a real window with a blue-sky view. Ideal for interior rooms, basements, or any space with no external windows.
