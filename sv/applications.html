Where YUERSky works

From windowless offices to hotel lobbies — artificial skylight transforms any interior into a bright, daylight-filled space, regardless of season.

Biophilic Design & Human-Centric Lighting

The power of bringing nature indoors

Access to natural light is the #1 attribute people seek in a workspace. Across the Nordic region, it is also the hardest to provide — until now.

🏢

Offices

Natural-quality overhead light boosts alertness, focus, and energy during the workday. Nordic workplace studies show up to 30% productivity gains with daylight-quality lighting.

  • Reduces afternoon energy slumps
  • Improves employee satisfaction
  • Lowers absenteeism during kaamos
🏥

Healthcare

Natural light in clinical settings reduces patient anxiety, shortens recovery times, and improves patient satisfaction scores — without requiring window access.

  • Waiting rooms & recovery wards
  • Windowless treatment rooms
  • Staff break rooms & corridors
🎓

Education

Daylight-quality lighting boosts student mood and concentration — especially critical in Nordic schools during the dark November–February period.

  • Classrooms & libraries
  • Gymnasiums & indoor spaces
  • Teacher workrooms
