The Problem

Finland's winters are beautiful — and brutal

Kaamos, the polar night, brings weeks of near-total darkness. For over a third of Finns, this means seasonal depression, disrupted sleep, and chronic fatigue.

Light therapy is the clinically proven solution. Our artificial skylight brings it directly into the architecture of a space — no lamp to switch on, no routine to remember. Just natural light, overhead, all day.
