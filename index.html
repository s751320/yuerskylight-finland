The Science

Your brain can't tell the difference

The human circadian system responds to light through intrinsically photosensitive retinal ganglion cells (ipRGCs) — not through conscious perception. This means that even when you know the sky above you is artificial, your body still responds as if it's real.

The key variables are: colour temperature (CCT), intensity (lux), and spectral quality (CRI). Our panels are engineered to hit all three — shifting from warm 2700K at dawn to bright 6500K at noon, with CRI > 90 to ensure the full spectrum reaches your eyes.

The result: your body clock runs on time, even in Helsinki in January.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Common questions from Finnish interior designers, architects, and project managers.

How much ceiling space is required for installation? ＋
The panels are slim-profile — typically only 5–8 cm of ceiling clearance is needed for recessed installation. They can also be surface-mounted with zero ceiling space required. Compatible with standard T-bar suspended ceiling grids used in most Finnish commercial buildings.
Are the panels CE certified for use in Finland and the EU? ＋
Yes. All YUERSky panels carry CE and RoHS certification, making them fully compliant for sale and installation across all EU member states including Finland. Full documentation — CE declaration of conformity, test reports, and RoHS certificates — is available on request before your purchase.
What is the shipping time to Finland? ＋
Standard lead time is 7 days for 1–2 units, and 10–15 days for larger project quantities. Shipment is by express courier (DHL/FedEx) directly to your address in Finland. Customs clearance and import duties may apply — we can advise on this before your order.
Can the colour temperature be dimmed or adjusted? ＋
Yes — the Smart version supports continuous CCT tuning from 2700K (warm sunrise) to 6500K (bright noon daylight), with 0–100% brightness dimming. Control is via Tuya WiFi app, BLE, or can integrate into existing DALI and 0-10V building management systems. A 24-hour automatic circadian rhythm mode is also available, which shifts the light throughout the day without manual adjustment.
What is the warranty? ＋
All panels come with a 3-year product warranty covering manufacturing defects. Panel lifespan is rated at 50,000 hours — equivalent to approximately 25 years of use at 6 hours per day.
Is it compatible with Finnish 230V electrical systems? ＋
Yes. All panels operate on 100–240V AC, 50/60Hz — fully compatible with the Finnish standard 230V supply. No additional transformer or adapter is required.
Can I order a sample before committing to a project quantity? ＋
Yes — we encourage it. Minimum order is 2 units. We recommend ordering one panel first to present to your client or test in a showroom before specifying for a full project. Just get in touch and we'll arrange a sample order with full documentation.
What sizes are available, and can custom sizes be made? ＋
Standard sizes are 300×300mm, 600×300mm, 600×600mm, 300×1200mm, and 600×1200mm. Custom sizes are available for larger project orders — please contact us with your specifications and we will confirm feasibility and lead time.
Does the panel look realistic? Can people tell it's artificial? ＋
The effect is designed to be visually convincing from normal viewing distances. The PMMA diffuser panel creates depth and a sense of natural light coming from above. At close inspection the panel is clearly artificial — but from a seated or standing position in a room, the psychological effect is strong. We recommend watching the installation videos on this page to judge for yourself.
Do you offer project pricing or volume discounts? ＋
Yes — we supply B2B partners with wholesale and project pricing. Discounts apply for orders of 5+ units. For ongoing reseller arrangements, we can discuss a distributor pricing structure. Contact us with your project scope and we will prepare a custom quotation.
